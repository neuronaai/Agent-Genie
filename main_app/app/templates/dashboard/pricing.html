Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I exceed my included minutes?

Calls continue uninterrupted. Overage minutes are billed at your plan's overage rate at the end of the billing period. You can also purchase top-up packs to prepay for additional minutes at a lower rate.

Can I change plans mid-cycle?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing period.

What happens to my agents if I cancel?

Your agents remain active until the end of the current billing period. After that, they are paused but not deleted. You can reactivate them by subscribing again.
